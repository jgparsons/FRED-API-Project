About the project

Fed Rate Watch is a student-built tool that tracks key Federal Reserve policy rates and turns them into simple visuals and daily email summaries.

What we built
  • Pulls data from the FRED API for ON RRP, EFFR, IORB, SOFR, and SRF.
  • Aggregates the last 30 days into a single chart for quick comparison.
  • Sends an email with today’s rates and the chart as an inline image.
Why we care about Fed rates

Policy rates shape borrowing costs for everything from credit cards and auto loans to business investment and housing. Understanding how they move is essential for anyone interested in macro, markets, or finance.

Our goal was to make this information easier to follow for students and early-career analysts who don’t have time to parse every FOMC release.
